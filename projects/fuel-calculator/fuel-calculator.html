NT Road Tip

The Central Arnhem Road from Katherine to Nhulunbuy is approximately 700 km — plan extra fuel stops as remote stations can be scarce.

NT Fuel Prices

Fuel prices in remote NT can be significantly higher than Darwin or Alice Springs. Budget an extra 20–40¢/L for outback travel.

4WD Tip

Many NT tracks require a 4WD vehicle. Fuel efficiency drops on unsealed roads — increase your estimate by 15–20% for dirt tracks.
